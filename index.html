About the Education School

Podar Education Network

Educating 2,30,000 students through 144 Podar International Schools, Podar Partner Schools, Podar Education Network has established itself as a leader in the education sector. Established in 1927, Podar Education Network’s aim is not just to impart knowledge but to foster the growth of responsible, well-rounded, and lifelong learners who can contribute positively to society.
